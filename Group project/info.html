Mahallah Asiah Introduction

بسم الله الرحمن الرحيم

Mahallah Asiah is one of the residential colleges at the International Islamic University Malaysia (IIUM). The mahallah is located on the main campus of IIUM, near the Kulliyyah of Engineering. It is named after Asiah bint Muzahim, the wife of the Pharaoh of Egypt at the time of Prophet Musa (AS) and the adoptive mother of Prophet Musa (AS). Mahallah Asiah was established in 2006. The mahallah consists of ten blocks in total that can accommodate up to 1200 students. Eight blocks for undergraduates, two blocks for postgraduates and one central building where the administration office, multipurpose hall, cafeteria, musolla, kiosks, MRC room and operation room are located.
